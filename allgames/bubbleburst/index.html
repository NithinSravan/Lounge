How to play?

  1. Click the bubbles quickly to burst them before they fill 75% of the screen.
  2. The speed of bubble generation increases with time so be quick.
  3. Pro-Tip: Burst the larger bubbles first in order to clear larger area.
  4. The longer you endure the higher the score!
  5. As game progress you will encounter rock bubbles. These are tough guys which require 5 clicks to break them.
  6. You have been given 2 Felix-Felicis which freezes the bubbles allowing you to pop bubbles by choosing them.Use them wisely!You can't click the Infinity Gauntlet while using this.
  7. Rock bubbles still have to be clicked 5 times to break them when using Felix-Felicis.
  8. The Infinity Gauntlet spawns rarely on screen.It clears 50% of the bubbles on the screen. Perfectly balanced as everything should be.
